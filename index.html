What is Interlasagna? +
A weather-aware Italian football fashion platform. Drop your city, the AI calls your kit like a proper gaffer. Pure calcio energy with a side of pasta.
How does The Gaffer AI work? +
We fetch live weather via WeatherAPI.com — temperature, conditions, humidity, wind. Our AI then calls your kit like a manager calls his starting XI. No negotiations. Andiamo.
What do the Italian kit names mean? +
Each kit is named after a football position in Italian style. Catenaccio = Defender (cold/rain). Regista = Midfielder (mild). Sole Mio = Attacker (sun/warm). Clean Sheet = Goalkeeper (rain). Bellissimo.
Are the kits real products? +
Yes! Every item is real football-inspired apparel, curated by the Gaffer based on your city's live weather. Built for the pitch and the piazza.
Why pasta? Why calcio? +
Pasta and calcio are the two greatest achievements of Italian civilisation. To dress like a gaffer is to embody both — structured, layered, always cooked al dente. Non c'è altro da dire.
